Department Overview

Cement & Concrete

Our Cement & Concrete department offers a wide range of high-quality cement and concrete products for all your construction needs. Whether you're working on a small home project or a large commercial build, we have the materials you need to get the job done right.

Lumber & Plywood

Our Lumber & Plywood department provides a variety of wood products, including dimensional lumber, plywood sheets, and specialty wood materials. We source our lumber from sustainable forests to ensure quality and environmental responsibility.

Steel Bars & Rebar

Our Steel Bars & Rebar department offers a selection of steel reinforcement products for concrete construction. We provide various sizes and grades of rebar to meet the structural requirements of your project.

Windows & Doors

Our Windows & Doors department features a wide range of products to enhance the aesthetics and functionality of your home or commercial space. We offer energy-efficient windows and durable doors in various styles and materials to suit your design preferences.

Water Tanks

Our Water Tanks department offers durable and reliable water storage solutions for residential, commercial, and agricultural use. We provide a variety of sizes and materials to meet your specific water storage needs.

Roofing Materials

Our Roofing Materials department provides a wide selection of roofing products, including shingles, metal roofing, and waterproof membranes. We offer materials that are designed to withstand harsh winds and rain, and provide long-lasting protection for your property.

Glass

Our Glass department offers a variety of glass products for both residential and commercial applications. From tempered glass to decorative options, we have the materials you need to enhance the aesthetics and functionality of your space.

Household Paints

Our Household Paints department features a wide range of interior and exterior paints in various colors and finishes. We offer high-quality paints that provide excellent coverage and durability for all your painting projects.

Electrical

Our Electrical department provides a comprehensive selection of electrical supplies, including wiring, outlets, switches, and lighting fixtures. We offer products that meet the latest safety standards to ensure your electrical projects are safe and efficient.

Plumbing Fixtures & Accessories

Our Plumbing Fixtures & Accessories department offers a variety of products for your plumbing needs, including faucets, sinks, toilets, and showerheads. We provide high-quality fixtures that combine functionality with stylish design to enhance your bathroom and kitchen spaces.
