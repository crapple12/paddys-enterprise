> About us

Established in 1993, Paddy's Enterprises & Hardware Ltd started as a small family business offering mainly roofing material. The owner, Franklin Paddy Roberts, initially created a shipping agency in 1992 under the name Paddy's Shipping and quickly expanded his hardware lines to include a wider variety of building materials, lumber and plywood, cement, concrete blocks and steel products, paints, doors and windows, even plumbing and electrical supplies, and gravel.

> Our Vision

To be the leading provider of hardware and building materials in the Caribbean, known for our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and community engagement.

Over the years, Paddy's Enterprises & Hardware Ltd has expanded to provide a wide range of goods and services unmatched by any other hardware supply business. Our expansion includes a 1500 sp. ft showroom on our top floor and our exclusive lines of Berger Paints. This growth reflects our dedication to meeting the evolving needs of our customers, and our customers immense trust in our business and its products.
